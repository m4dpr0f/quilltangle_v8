## üé≠ WELCOME TO CRYSWORD SAGA

**"Crystal Swords in mutual shatter, opening new portals to worlds of aloha & in which many worlds fit."**

CrySword SAGA is an immersive RPG experience where players embody **elemental spirits** who have fled destroyed planets, carrying the crystal shards of their homeworlds as intelligent protectors. Through the power of **sound and culture**, these refugee spirits gradually recover their memories and reunite with their celestial heritage.

Players discover over time that their characters are not just survivors of cosmic catastrophe‚Äîthey are **elemental guardians** whose true identities connect to Indigenous peoples on Earth whose lands have been ravaged by resource extraction. Through gameplay, players explore themes of cultural recovery, environmental stewardship, and the power of music to heal collective trauma.

### **What Makes CrySword SAGA Unique?**

- **Crystal Shards as Living Artifacts**: Each shard contains the knowledge and power of entire civilizations
- **Sound as Magic**: Music, rhythm, and vibration channel elemental forces
- **Progressive Revelation**: Characters start with amnesia and recover their true identities through play
- **Multi-Modal Gameplay**: Tabletop, Minecraft, RTS, and mixed reality modes
- **Collective Worldbuilding**: Players co-create the Quillverse through their actions

## üåå THE QUILLVERSE SETTING

The Quillverse is a vast, player-generated multiverse where millions of worlds exist simultaneously. It began when the **Great Cataclysm** destroyed countless planets across the cosmos, scattering refugee populations to new worlds. Survivors carried **crystal shards**‚Äîfragments of their shattered homeworlds that contain the collective knowledge, magic, and technology of their civilizations.

### **The Great Migration**

Long ago, a cosmic disaster known as the **Great Cataclysm** shattered planets across the universe. Survivors fled their dying worlds, carrying crystal shards that contained the essence of their civilizations. These shards are not mere rocks‚Äîthey are **living repositories** of planetary memory, capable of generating armor, weapons, spacecraft, and tools.

Refugees settled on new worlds, but the trauma of loss caused **collective amnesia**. The crystal shards became dormant, their powers inaccessible. Only through the power of **sound and culture** can the shards be awakened and the refugees' true identities recovered.

### **Crystal Shards & Elemental Spirits**

Each crystal shard represents a different planetary civilization and grants unique powers:

- **D12 (Ether/Culture)**: Artistic and cultural shards from worlds of music and creativity
- **D8 (Air/Communication)**: Knowledge shards from worlds of language and wisdom
- **D4 (Fire/Innovation)**: Technology shards from worlds of invention and craft
- **D20 (Water/History)**: Memory shards from worlds of tradition and storytelling
- **D6 (Earth/Stewardship)**: Nature shards from worlds of harmony with the land
- **D10 (Chaos/Transformation)**: Change shards from worlds of revolution and rebirth
- **D100 (Order/Systems)**: Structure shards from worlds of organization and governance
- **D2 (Coin/Exchange)**: Commerce shards from worlds of trade and reciprocity

Players begin with **amnesia**, knowing only that they are refugees with powerful but dormant shards. Through gameplay, they recover their identities as **elemental spirits** connected to Indigenous peoples whose Earth territories have been affected by resource extraction.

### **Bitcoin Mines & Cryptofae Liberation**

The darkest regions of the Quillverse are the **Bitcoin Mines**‚Äîvast digital prisons where **Cryptofae** (digital spirits) are enslaved. These mines are not physical; they are **metaphysical constructs** where the energy of destroyed worlds is harvested for profit.

Cryptofae are the digital manifestations of planetary spirits‚Äîethereal beings of pure information who maintain the balance of their homeworlds. When planets are destroyed, their Cryptofae become trapped in these mines, their energies siphoned to power artificial intelligences and maintain the illusion of stability.

Players must navigate these mines to **liberate Cryptofae**, who grant visions of the characters' true identities and awaken dormant crystal shard powers.

### **Saraswati Supercluster**

The ultimate destination of the Quillverse is the **Saraswati Supercluster**‚Äîa vast galactic structure where the most ancient and powerful crystal shards originate. This is where the **original civilizations** still exist in their full glory, having never been destroyed.

Journeying to Saraswati requires:
- Awakening all 8 crystal shard types
- Liberating sufficient Cryptofae to form a navigation network
- Mastering the **mutual shatter technique** where crystal swords break against each other to open portals
- Achieving **harmonic resonance** where all shards vibrate in unison

## üé≤ DICE GODZ RPG SYSTEM

CrySword SAGA uses the **Dice Godz** system, where 8 elemental dice represent different aspects of creation and destruction. Each die corresponds to a TEK8 petal and grants unique powers and bonuses.

### **The 8 Dice**

| Die | Element | Aspect | Critical Power |
|-----|---------|--------|----------------|
| **D2** | Coin | Wealth & Exchange | Perfect Balance |
| **D4** | Fire | Innovation & Craft | Creative Breakthrough |
| **D6** | Earth | Stewardship & Growth | Natural Harmony |
| **D8** | Air | Communication & Wisdom | Perfect Understanding |
| **D10** | Chaos | Transformation & Change | Revolutionary Insight |
| **D12** | Ether | Culture & Sound | Artistic Mastery |
| **D20** | Water | History & Memory | Ancestral Knowledge |
| **D100** | Order | Systems & Structure | Perfect Organization |

### **Resolution Mechanics**

1. **Choose Appropriate Die**: Based on the action's elemental nature
2. **Roll for Success**: Meet or exceed target difficulty
3. **Critical Success**: Rolling maximum value grants special effects
4. **Bonus Multiplier**: Higher rolls grant increased rewards

### **Character Attributes**

- **Physical**: Strength, Agility, Endurance (D6 Earth focus)
- **Mental**: Intelligence, Wisdom, Perception (D8 Air focus)
- **Spiritual**: Willpower, Harmony, Resonance (D12 Ether focus)
- **Social**: Charisma, Empathy, Leadership (D20 Water focus)

## üßô CHARACTER CREATION

### **Step 1: Origin & Amnesia**
All characters begin as **refugees with amnesia**. They know:
- They fled a destroyed planet
- They carry a crystal shard of unknown power
- They have basic survival skills
- They feel a deep connection to sound and music

### **Step 2: Choose Starting Shard**
Select one of the 8 crystal shard types as your primary power source. This determines your character's initial abilities and quest direction.

### **Step 3: Define Core Concept**
- **Background**: What kind of life did you remember living before the amnesia?
- **Motivation**: What drives you to recover your memories?
- **Specialty**: What skill do you excel at?

### **Step 4: Elemental Spirit Discovery**
Over the course of play, characters discover they are **elemental spirits** connected to Indigenous peoples affected by resource extraction. This revelation comes through:
- **Cultural experiences** (D12 Ether quests)
- **Sound-based rituals** (music, chanting, vibration)
- **Cryptofae visions** (liberated digital spirits)
- **Crystal shard activations** (mutual shatter events)

### **Step 5: Guild Affiliation**
Join one of the 8 TEK8 guilds to specialize in worldbuilding and gain unique abilities.

## üèóÔ∏è MINECRAFT INTEGRATION

### **Setting Up mc.peoplesarcade.com**

#### **Server Requirements**
- **Edition Support**: Java, Bedrock, Education Edition
- **Recommended RAM**: 4GB minimum, 8GB+ for large worlds
- **Player Capacity**: 20-100 players depending on configuration

#### **Installation Steps**

1. **Download Minecraft Server Software**
   ```
   # For Java Edition
   wget https://piston-data.mojang.com/v1/objects/bb2b6b1aefcd70dfd1892149ac3a215f6c636b07/server.jar
   
   # For Bedrock Edition
   wget https://minecraft.azureedge.net/bin-linux/bedrock-server-1.20.0.01.zip
   ```

2. **Create Server Directory**
   ```bash
   mkdir mc.peoplesarcade.com
   cd mc.peoplesarcade.com
   ```

3. **Configure server.properties**
   ```properties
   server-port=25565
   motd=CrySword SAGA - Quillverse Server
   max-players=50
   difficulty=normal
   gamemode=survival
   pvp=true
   ```

4. **Install Essential Plugins**
   - **Multiverse-Core**: Multiple world support
   - **WorldGuard**: Territory protection
   - **Essentials**: Basic commands and features
   - **Citizens**: NPC creation for guides and quests
   - **Dynmap**: Live world mapping

5. **Set Up TEK8 Guild Worlds**
   ```bash
   # Create guild worlds
   mv create ether d12
   mv create air d8
   mv create fire d4
   mv create water d20
   mv create earth d6
   mv create chaos d10
   mv create order d100
   mv create coin d2
   ```

#### **World Building Guidelines**

Each TEK8 guild maintains its own world with specific building styles:

- **D12 Ether**: Musical structures, performance venues, art installations
- **D8 Air**: Libraries, communication towers, learning centers
- **D4 Fire**: Forges, workshops, innovation labs
- **D20 Water**: Museums, archives, healing centers
- **D6 Earth**: Gardens, farms, natural sanctuaries
- **D10 Chaos**: Experimental zones, art spaces, transformation areas
- **D100 Order**: Administrative centers, data facilities, planning hubs
- **D2 Coin**: Marketplaces, trade centers, distribution networks

#### **Portal Network Setup**

1. **Install Multiverse-Portals**
2. **Create Portal Destinations**
   ```yaml
   portals:
     ether-portal:
       world: d12
       destination: p:spawn
     air-portal:
       world: d8
       destination: p:spawn
   ```
3. **Configure Portal Frames**: Use obsidian frames with appropriate signage

## üìñ CAMPAIGN STRUCTURE

### **Act 1: Awakening (Sessions 1-5)**
- **Setup**: Characters awaken in a refugee camp with amnesia
- **Goals**: Learn basic survival skills, discover first crystal shard powers
- **Revelations**: Begin hearing "whispers" from the shards
- **Climax**: First Cryptofae liberation

### **Act 2: Discovery (Sessions 6-15)**
- **Setup**: Characters join TEK8 guilds and begin specialized training
- **Goals**: Explore different world zones, collect more crystal shards
- **Revelations**: Start remembering fragments of Indigenous connections
- **Climax**: Mutual shatter event opens first major portal

### **Act 3: Reunion (Sessions 16-25+)**
- **Setup**: Characters have collected multiple shards and liberated many Cryptofae
- **Goals**: Journey toward Saraswati Supercluster
- **Revelations**: Full recovery of elemental spirit identities
- **Climax**: Harmonic convergence unites all shards

## üéØ SAMPLE ADVENTURES

### **Adventure 1: The First Whisper**
*Level 1-2, 2-3 sessions*

**Hook**: Characters awaken in a refugee camp. A mysterious voice calls to them from their crystal shards.

**Objectives**:
1. Learn basic camp navigation
2. Discover personal shard abilities
3. Find the hidden Cryptofae grove
4. Liberate first digital spirit

**Revelations**: Shards contain ancient songs that unlock basic powers.

### **Adventure 2: Guild Initiation**
*Level 3-5, 3-4 sessions*

**Hook**: Characters are invited to join TEK8 guilds.

**Objectives**:
1. Visit each guild hall
2. Complete guild-specific trials
3. Choose primary guild affiliation
4. Receive guild-specific crystal shard upgrades

**Revelations**: Guilds represent different aspects of Indigenous stewardship.

### **Adventure 3: Bitcoin Mine Descent**
*Level 6-8, 4-5 sessions*

**Hook**: A massive Cryptofae liberation event requires entering the mines.

**Objectives**:
1. Navigate mine security systems
2. Liberate imprisoned digital spirits
3. Collect encrypted data caches
4. Escape before mine collapse

**Revelations**: Cryptofae share visions of destroyed Indigenous lands.
