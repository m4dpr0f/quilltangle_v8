# CrySword SAGA: A Quilltangle-Inspired Tabletop RPG

## Overview

CrySword SAGA is a tabletop RPG adaptation of our Quilltangle: Pixie Parable MMORPG, reimagining the pixel-perfect typing mechanics as mystical crystal shard resonance and transforming our faction progression system into a crystal-sword wielding multiverse adventure.

## Core Concept: From Pixels to Crystals

**Quilltangle System → CrySword Adaptation:**
- **Typing Speed/Accuracy** → **Crystal Resonance Mastery**
- **8 Faction Progression (D2-D100)** → **8 Crystal Sword Schools**
- **XP from Characters Typed** → **Mana from Word-Weaving Challenges**
- **Sacred Tomes** → **Planetary Shard Codices**
- **Citizens Assembly** → **Refugee Council Democracy**

## Core Mechanics: Word-Weaving Resonance

### Crystal Resonance Tests
Replace our typing speed mechanics with Word-Weaving challenges:

**Basic Resonance Test:**
1. GM presents a scenario or challenge
2. Player chooses their approach based on their Crystal Sword school
3. Player must articulate their action in exactly the word count matching their die type:
   - D2: 2 words (maximum clarity)
   - D4: 4 words (passionate declaration) 
   - D6: 6 words (steady foundational statement)
   - D8: 8 words (rhythmic harmonic expression)
   - D10: 10 words (innovative adaptive description)
   - D12: 12 words (mystical ethereal invocation)
   - D20: 20 words (fluid emotional narrative)
   - D100: 100 words (complete encyclopedic analysis)

**Resonance Success:**
- Player rolls their faction die
- Success threshold determined by challenge difficulty
- Additional successes based on how well their word-weaving matches their Crystal Sword's nature

### Mana System (Adapted from XP Calculator)
**Base Mana Gain**: 8 points per successful challenge
**Word Resonance Bonus**: +1 mana per word in successful challenge × position multiplier
**Campaign Bonus**: +25 mana + (10 × chapter number) for major story moments

**Position Multipliers** (adapted from our racing system):
- Leading the group: 100% bonus
- Supporting role: 50% bonus  
- Contributing role: 33% bonus
- Observing role: 25% bonus

## Refugee Council Democracy (Citizens Assembly Adaptation)

### Assembly Mechanics
Our digital democracy system becomes face-to-face council sessions:

**Proposal Creation**: Any player can propose solutions to refugee crises using their Crystal Sword school's approach
**Resonance Voting**: Players "vote" by articulating support using their word-count limits
**Harmonic Convergence**: When proposals from different schools combine beautifully, bonus effects activate
**Musical Translation**: GMs can hum or sing the "harmony" of successful collaborative decisions

### Democratic Challenge Resolution
**Crisis Scenarios**: Food shortages, territorial disputes, resource allocation, defense planning
**Multi-School Solutions**: Challenges requiring coordination between different Crystal Sword approaches
**Consensus Building**: Success measured by how well different schools harmonize their approaches

CrySword SAGA takes our digital typing game's core insights about learning through challenge, faction diversity, and democratic collaboration, and transforms them into a rich tabletop experience where word-mastery becomes mystical power, typing speed becomes crystal resonance, and our Sacred Tomes become the foundation for rebuilding civilization itself.

Players don't just type words—they weave reality. They don't just race—they create harmony. They don't just accumulate XP—they gather the mana needed to heal a shattered multiverse.

The refugees of CrySword SAGA carry forward the same spirit that drives our Quilltangle citizens: that through language, community, and mutual respect, we can build worlds that honor the best of what was lost while creating something entirely new.
